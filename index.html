Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

How do I register my baby born in UK as an Indian citizen?
You need to file Form I on indiancitizenshiponline.nic.in, then visit the Indian High Commission with originals. Sorted handles the entire online process for you for £120 — just WhatsApp us your documents and we take care of everything. Learn more about Form I →
How long does OCI card take from UK?
Typically 6-8 weeks from VFS submission to receiving your OCI card. Sorted ensures your application is submitted cleanly to avoid delays from rejections or queries. Learn more about OCI →
What documents do I need to send you?
For Form I: both parents' passport bio-data pages, baby's full UK birth certificate, and marriage certificate. For OCI and other services, requirements vary — WhatsApp us and we'll send you a personalised checklist.
How do I pay?
We use secure Stripe payment links. You can pay by credit or debit card. For Form I and OCI, you can pay directly from our website. No cash, no bank transfers needed.
Do you only serve Sheffield?
No — we serve NRI families across the entire UK. Everything is done via WhatsApp, so it doesn't matter where you are. We have clients in London, Birmingham, Manchester, Leeds, Glasgow, Edinburgh, Bristol, Newcastle, Cardiff, and many other cities.
Do I still need to visit the High Commission in person?
Yes, at least one parent needs to visit the Indian High Commission or Consulate with original documents. But Sorted prepares everything — your completed form, document package, and a day-of-appointment guide — so your visit is quick and stress-free.
