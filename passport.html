Indian passport renewal made simple

Renewing your Indian passport in the UK means navigating the Passport Seva portal, booking an appointment through BLS International, preparing the right documents, and attending your appointment with everything in order. Each step has its own quirks — the BLS website is notoriously difficult to find available slots on, and the passport application form has fields that confuse even experienced applicants.

Sorted streamlines this entire process. We complete your online application on passportindia.gov.in, secure your BLS appointment, prepare your document package, and give you a clear checklist for your appointment day. All for £100.

The BLS appointment challenge

The single biggest frustration NRIs face with passport renewal is booking a BLS International appointment. Slots are released in batches and fill up within minutes. Many people spend weeks refreshing the BLS website trying to find an available slot.

Sorted knows when new BLS slots are typically released and monitors availability. We usually secure your appointment within 1–2 days of new slots opening, saving you weeks of frustration.

Passport FAQ

Common passport renewal questions

How do I renew my Indian passport in the UK?
You apply online through the Passport Seva portal (passportindia.gov.in), book a BLS International appointment, then attend with original documents. Sorted handles the online application and appointment booking for £100.
How do I book a BLS Indian passport appointment?
BLS appointments are booked through the BLS International portal. Slots are released periodically and fill up within minutes. Sorted monitors slot availability and books your appointment quickly, usually within a day of slots opening.
How long does Indian passport renewal take?
Standard processing takes 3–4 weeks from your BLS appointment to receiving your new passport by post. Tatkal (urgent) processing can be completed in under 2 weeks for an additional government fee.
Can I change my name on my Indian passport after marriage?
Yes. Name changes require additional documentation including your marriage certificate, a deed poll (if applicable), and an affidavit. The form and process differ from a standard renewal — Sorted handles all the extra requirements.
How do I apply for my child's first Indian passport?
Your child's birth must be registered first via Form I. Once you have the birth registration certificate, the passport application can be filed through Passport Seva. It's a different process from adult renewal. Sorted handles the full Form I → passport sequence.
What if my Indian passport is damaged?
Damaged passports require a re-issue application rather than standard renewal. You may need a police report depending on the damage. Sorted guides you through this specific process.
Is tatkal (urgent) passport renewal available in UK?
Yes, tatkal processing is available for genuinely urgent cases such as medical emergencies or bereavement travel. It costs more at the government level but significantly reduces processing time. Sorted helps you assess eligibility.
How much does Indian passport renewal cost?
Sorted charges £100 for the complete service including BLS appointment booking. Government/BLS fees of approximately £20–40 are payable separately at your appointment. Total cost: approximately £120–140.
