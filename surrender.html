Why do I need to surrender my Indian passport?

Under Indian law, if you've acquired citizenship of another country (like British citizenship), you must surrender your Indian passport and obtain a Surrender Certificate. This certificate is then required before you can apply for an OCI card.

Many people don't realise this is a separate step — you can't just apply for OCI directly. The surrender must happen first, and you need the certificate in hand before the OCI application can begin.
